Prof. Qianqian Wang

Professor and Associate Dean, School of Mechanical Engineering, SEU

Director of Management Committee, Jiangsu Key Lab for Design and Manufacturing of Precision Medicine Equipment

National High-level Young Scholar Program (overseas)

1st Xiaomi Young Scholar

MINE Outstanding Young Scientist

Qianqian Wang is currently a Professor and Associate Dean (Faculty&External Affairs) at the School of Mechanical Engineering, Southeast University (SEU), Nanjing, China. He received his Ph.D. in Mechanical and Automation Engineering (MAE) at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2020. Before joining SEU, he was a Postdoctoral Fellow affiliated with Department of MAE, CUHK.

He has published more than 40 papers in peer-reviewed journals, including Science Robotics, Science Advances, Nature Communications, and IEEE Transactions series, and presented his work in IEEE ICRA, IROS.etc. Several of his journal papers have been indexed as ESI Highly Cited Papers and Hot Papers (Top 0.1%) and have been highlighted in Nature News and international press reports. He has published two monographs with Springer Nature and CRC Press. His research focuses on the medical-engineering Interdisciplinary microrobotic systems, including collective behaviors of micro/nanorobots, medical imaging-guided delivery, and intelligent control. He is an IEEE Senior Member and Associate Editor of IEEE T-ASE, IROS, ICRA.

Prof. Zhiqiang Tang

Associate Professor, School of Mechanical Engineering, SEU

Jiangsu Key Lab for Design and Manufacturing of Precision Medicine Equipment

Medical Robotics

Soft Robotics

Robot Learning and Control

Zhiqiang Tang is currently an Associate Professor at the School of Mechanical Engineering, Southeast University (SEU), Nanjing, China. He received his Ph.D. in Electronic Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2021. Before joining SEU, he was a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at National University of Singapore (NUS) and Singapore-MIT Alliance for Research and Technology (SMART) Centre.

His research focuses on robot learning and control, soft robots, embodied intelligence, and medical robots. He has published more than 20 papers in peer-reviewed journals, including The International Journal of Robotics Research, Soft Robotics, IEEE Transactions series, and presented his work in IEEE ICRA, IROS.
